Features

Everything in one place

💰

Finance

Import XLS, PDF, or TXT bank statements. Auto-categorise transactions, track budgets, goals, debts, and net worth.

❤️

Health

Log daily metrics, connect Google Fit, set goals and track trends. Custom metrics with any name, unit, and color.

✅

Tasks

One-off and recurring tasks — daily, weekly, or monthly. Overdue tasks always stay visible.

🔁

Habits

Daily habit tracking alongside your health metrics. Check off from the Home screen or Health → Today.

📱

Desktop + Phone

Install as a PWA on your phone and access from any device on your local network or via Tailscale.

Everything runs locally. No telemetry, no analytics, no cloud sync. Your data never leaves your machine.
