Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-05  ·  Last updated: 2026-06-05

Overview

Meridian is a local-only personal organiser for finance, health, tasks, and habits. All data you enter is stored exclusively on your own device. Meridian does not operate servers that receive, store, or process your personal information.

Data we do not collect

  • No account registration — there is no Meridian cloud account.
  • No analytics or usage telemetry.
  • No advertising identifiers or third-party tracking.
  • No crash reports sent to Meridian servers.

Data stored locally on your device

Everything you enter into Meridian — transactions, budgets, health metrics, tasks, habits, and any imported bank statements — is stored in a SQLite database on your own machine (%APPDATA%\Meridian\ on Windows, or a Docker volume you control). This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export a backup file.

Session cookie

Meridian sets a single session cookie to keep you logged in. This cookie is stored only in your local browser and is never transmitted to any Meridian server.

Google Fit integration (optional)

If you connect Google Fit, Meridian uses Google's OAuth 2.0 flow to request read-only access to your fitness data. The OAuth token is stored locally on your device. Health data fetched from Google Fit is written to your local database and is not transmitted anywhere else. You can disconnect Google Fit at any time from Settings → Health.

Push notifications (optional)

If you enable push notifications, your browser generates a push subscription endpoint (provided by your browser vendor's push service — e.g. Google FCM for Chrome). This endpoint is stored locally and used only to deliver notifications from your own Meridian instance to your own device. Meridian does not use it for any other purpose.

Software updates

The Electron desktop app checks for updates via GitHub Releases (github.com/LfmaSL/Meridian). This check sends a standard HTTPS request to GitHub's servers (subject to GitHub's Privacy Statement). No personal data from inside the app is included in this request.

Payment processing

License purchases are processed by Paddle.com Market Limited, acting as Merchant of Record. When you purchase a license, you interact directly with Paddle's checkout — Meridian does not receive or store your payment details. Paddle's handling of your payment data is governed by Paddle's Privacy Policy.

Your rights

Because Meridian stores data only on your device, you have full control at all times. You can export, delete, or reset all data from Settings → Data. There is no cloud account to close.

Contact

Questions about this policy: privacy@yourdomain.com
